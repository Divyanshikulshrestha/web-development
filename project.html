Financial Tips for College Students

1. Create a monthly budget and stick to it.

2. Save a portion of your allowance or part-time job income.

3. Avoid unnecessary expenses, like eating out frequently.

4. Consider opening a savings account for long-term goals.

5. Explore scholarship and internship opportunities.

6. Invest in your education and future career.

7. Plan for student loan repayment if applicable.
